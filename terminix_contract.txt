TERMINIX SERVICE AGREEMENT & CONTRACT
Agreement Number: TX-90812-C
Date: October 14, 2025
Customer: Jane Doe
Property Address: 742 Evergreen Terrace, Springfield

1. PEST CONTROL PLAN DETAILS
This agreement provides preventative and reactive pest control services for the designated property. Standard covered pests include: Common Household Ants, Cockroaches, Mice, Rats, Silverfish, Earwigs, and Centipedes.
Excluded Pests: Termites (Reticulitermes spp., Coptotermes spp.), Bedbugs, Mosquitoes, Fleas, Wood-boring beetles, and Honeybees. Treatment for excluded pests requires a separate specialized agreement.

2. SERVICE SCHEDULE & TREATMENT AREA
- Service Frequency: Quarterly (once every three months).
- Treatment Areas: Exterior perimeter barrier (up to 3 feet up the foundation wall and 3 feet out), interior baseboards (kitchen, bathrooms, utility areas), garage, and crawlspace.
- Chemical Agents: Demand CS (Lambda-cyhalothrin 9.7%) and Suspend PolyZone (Deltamethrin 4.75%).

3. TERMINIX SERVICE GUARANTEE
If covered pests return between scheduled quarterly visits, Terminix will return to treat the property at no additional cost within 48 hours of notification. The customer must maintain standard property sanitation (removing standing water, sealing food containers) for the guarantee to remain in effect.

4. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Terminix is responsible only for applying chemical agents in accordance with EPA labels. Terminix does not guarantee the elimination of all pests, nor does it accept liability for:
- Medical expenses resulting from pest bites, stings, or disease transmission.
- Any property damage or structural wood damage caused by termites, carpenter ants, or other wood-destroying insects (whether covered or excluded).
- Damages arising from customer's failure to allow access to treatment areas.
In no event shall Terminix's total liability exceed the annual amount paid by the customer under this agreement.

5. TERM AND CANCELLATION
- Initial Term: 12 months.
- Renewal: Automatically renews month-to-month after the initial term.
- Cancellation Policy: Either party may cancel with 30 days written notice. If the customer cancels prior to the end of the initial 12-month term, an early termination fee of $150.00 will be assessed.

6. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
Any controversy or claim arising out of or relating to this contract, or the breach thereof, shall be settled by binding arbitration administered by the American Arbitration Association (AAA) in accordance with its Commercial Arbitration Rules.
